# Atlas Post-Showing Call Feedback Rubric (v1)
**Scale:** 1 (Poor) — 5 (Excellent)  
**Weights:** Clarity 20% · Empathy 15% · Resolution 25% · Actionability 25% · Efficiency 15%

## 1) Clarity (20%)
1 — Vague; needs follow-ups  
3 — Understandable but missing specifics  
5 — Concrete likes/dislikes with examples (eg, “street noise ~6–8pm”)

## 2) Empathy (15%)
1 — Dismissive; ignores concerns  
3 — Neutral acknowledgment  
5 — Reflects buyer’s feelings; validates concerns without overpromising

## 3) Resolution (25%)
1 — No next steps; open issues remain  
3 — Partial resolutions proposed  
5 — Clear next steps (alt listings, schedule follow-up, send comps)

## 4) Actionability (25%)
1 — Not usable by agent/seller  
3 — Some actionable notes  
5 — Specific, assignable tasks with owners and timing

## 5) Efficiency (15%)
1 — Long/rambling (>8 min), low signal  
3 — Average pacing (3–5 min)  
5 — Concise (≤3 min), high signal density

**Buckets to store in DB:** interest level, likes, dislikes, agent requested, offer intent, info requests, follow-ups.
